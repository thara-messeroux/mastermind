Mastermind

How to play

  1. Pick 4 different colors (no duplicates).
  2. Click Submit Guess.
  3. Feedback pegs are aligned by position:
    • Black = that position is correct.
    • White = that position is not correct.
  4. Win by getting 4 black pegs.

Testing: click Reveal Code or press C.
